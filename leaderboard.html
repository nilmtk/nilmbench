How to read the board

Ranking is cohort-specific.

One cohort at a time

Task, appliance, resolution, data access, scope, profile, and sample limit define who can be ranked together.

Smoke is not full

Smoke runs check the real pipeline cheaply. Only complete full-protocol runs support benchmark or paper claims.

Three required seeds

Verified aggregates require seeds 10, 20, and 42 plus source, dataset, runtime, container, and protocol provenance.
